### Response

Hi There,

Thank you for reaching out to us. 

Redirecting your `/blog` path to `https://example.com` on Vercel is a straightforward process. 

Please follow the steps below:

1. Log in to your Vercel account and navigate to the project you want to redirect.
2. Click on the "Settings" tab.
3. Select "Redirects" from the menu.
4. Click on "Add Redirect" and fill in the details as follows:
    - Source: `/blog`
    - Destination: `https://example.com`
    - Permanent: Yes
5. Click on "Save" to apply the redirect.

After completing these steps, any user who visits the `/blog` path on your Vercel project will be automatically redirected to `https://example.com`.

If you have any further questions or need additional assistance, feel free to reach out. We're here to help!
